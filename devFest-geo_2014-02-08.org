* Intro

- Brief overview of geo-spatial miscellany in R
- Key libraries
  - Working with Google maps: =ggmap=
  - Great circle paths: =geosphere=
  - Larger scale maps: =maps= (comes with R)
  - Projection conversion/KML generation: =rgdal= and =sp=
  - Plotting: =ggplot2=
